Hi, I am Sanjida. I am going to share my travel blog. Recently I have visited Sylhet. It was a great journey. I have fallen in love with nature.

Nestled in the pictureque hills of northeastern Bangladesh, Sylhet is a traveler's paradise. Known for its lush tea gardens, stunning waterfalls, and the serene Ratargul Swamp Forest, Sylhet offers a traquil escape from city life. Explore the spiritual haven of Hazrat shah Jalal's shrine, enjoy the mesmerizing views from Jaflong's stone collections, and wander through the enchanting Lalakhal river with its emerland waters. Don't miss the opportunity to taste Sylheti cuisine, with its unique blend of flavors.
